Credits




Super Smash Bros Melee by Nintendo was made and played to be a party game. While millions played free-for-all with items on full, a certain few turned items off and started going grit for grit one-on-one. Analysing frame data of their own character and their opponent's can help formulate tactics that can lead to victory.

Frame Melee is a general information mobile application that looks at Super Smash Bros Melee and its array of characters frame by frame. After choosing one of the game's 26 playable characters, you can select any one of his or her smashes, aerials, attacks, or specials to analyze. It contains what frame hitboxes come out, where the vulnerable hurtbox are, and if there are any invulnerabilities to look out for.
